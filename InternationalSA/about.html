About Us

Welcome!
I moved to America when I was 18 and to say I had no idea what to expect is an understatement.

I've spent a lot of time trying to research and understand this experience. Whilst there are many websites for international students or athletes in college sports, there was never anything specifically for an International Student Athlete. This was surprising considering the large population of individuals who move to America to study and compete.

I struggled to find anything specific, and the most realistic information I found was watching A Day In The Life on YouTube. I still find myself today searching through endless websites, trying to piece information together.
